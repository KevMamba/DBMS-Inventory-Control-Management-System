  • About our team

    The Warehouse is a Bangalore based startup that intends to revolutinize the way inventry control and management functions globally. Inspired by the expeditious and seemlessly smooth inventory management systems of Walmart and Amazon, The Warehouse too intends to amplify their idealogies and techniques and be a game changer in the field of inventory control management systems.

    Beginnings

    The Warehouse started by two sophomore students in PES University's Computer Science Department, Sriram SK and Kevin Arulraj. Under the guidance of their professor Mrs. Mamatha HR were they able to establish the database management systems of the company.
